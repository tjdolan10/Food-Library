Title: Mayo

Source:

Yield: 1 Cup
Active: 5Min
Total: 
Categories: condiment
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/4909ac50e69445409eca08e3a486a228.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 Egg
	1 TB Mustard
	1 TB Vinegar
	Pinch of salt
	1 Cup Oil
	1 Lemon

Instructions:
	Mix egg, mustard, vinegar, and salt
	Extremely slowly at first, drizzle in oil.  Once emulsification happens, drizzle in rest of oil while whisking.
	Lemon to taste.
