Title: Hard Boiled Eggs
Description: 
Source: 
Original URL: 
Yield: 12
Active: 5 Minutes
Total: 30 Minutes
Categories: toppings, sides, breakfast
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/893a677455174d6da722ac2034bd3847.jpg
Ingredients:
	Dozen Eggs

Instructions:
	Pot big enough for eggs not to overlap. Fill 1-2 inches above the eggs with cold water.
	Bring water to boil. Immediately turn off heat. Let rest for 10-12 minutes.
	Remove eggs from heat and run cold water over to stop cooking.
